## Notes from reading them (for the reserves chapter)

- **Reserve funds** (chart of accounts A814–A884) are *restricted* fund balances,
  each established under a specific statute for a specific purpose — e.g.
  Capital Reserve (Ed Law §3651), Tax Certiorari (GML §3651.1-a, mandatory),
  Retirement Contribution ERS/TRS (GML §6-r), Employee Benefits (GML §6-p),
  Insurance (GML §6-m), Repairs (GML §6-d), Unemployment Insurance, Workers' Comp.
- **Fund balance** (A913–A917) is classified GASB 54-style: Committed, Assigned
  (appropriated / unappropriated), and **Unassigned (A917)** — the residual,
  general-purpose balance.
- **The 4% cap** applies to the *unassigned / unexpended-surplus* fund balance
  (AT0994), not to the restricted reserve funds. So a district may hold large
  restricted reserves and still be "at the cap" on its flexible balance — which
  is the key relationship the chapter explains.
